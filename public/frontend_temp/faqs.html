Faq

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, see our support center.

Who can invest?
Any US resident, unaccredited or accredited.
Is there an age limit?
You must be 18 years of age to open an account, but we encourage kids to get involved under their parents account by creating a sub account. There is not fee for this kind of account if the account has less than $5000 in it
Are their limits in how much I can invest?
No. This is the beautiful thing about our technology. Historically, it would have been very difficult to get into this kind of investment and manage it. With technology, we provide you with the tools to participate and make management decisions (actually, it is required)
Can international investors invest?
Not at this time. We are working on this and hope to have this as an option soon.
Can I invest with my retirement account?
No. This is not considered an investment vehicle. When you purchase an ownership in any property, you are literally entering into a joint venture with others. Much like starting your own business.
What is the minimum amount I can invest?
We have limited the amount at this point to $100 due to fees and limited returns once you go under this amount
Can I add to my investment?
Yep! Anytime you like. Just look for a property you like and feel comfortable with and make an offer
Can I use my credit card to purchase ownership?
No. We developed this model to be strictly only. This is really to protect your investment
Why don?t you allow for mortgages on any of the properties on your platform?
We developed this model to be strictly . While we understand the value and power of leverage, we also understand the risks and feel it is best to avoid this kind of investment model.
How do I sell my ownership?
We have provided a secondary marketplace on our platform to allow for all members to sell their ownership. Ownership is limited to those who have taken our knowledge base test and have past
Do I have to be a member on the platform in order to participate?
No. Our platform was created to enable you to manage your ownership and make decisions easier. You can still be an owner and get the important information via email. The benefit of our platform offers is being able to have access to the marketplace, dashboards and documents real time. However, we do require everyone participating in ownership of on our platform to take our test so we can verify that you understand the process. This is a business
Why do you ask for my SSN when I sign up?
Since you are an owner in an LLC, we will provide you a schedule K1 at the end of the year. This document requires that we include your SSN in this document for tax reporting purposes.
How do I report my income on my taxes?
Since you will be an owner of the property owning LLC, you will receive a schedule K1 for every property you have had ownership in for that tax year. You can then provide that schedule to your tax professional.
What am I buying when I purchase ?ownership? in a property?
You are purchasing ownership in the LLC that owns the property. This is how you are able to sell your ownership easily without having to go through the process of transferring title and all of the paperwork associated with purchasing a property.
What if our LLC would like to sell our property?
If there is a request and a vote put forward to all owners and the owners approve of a sale by a super majority (80%), then the property will be listed for sale through our realtor. You will continue to own and operate the same way until the property has been purchased. Once the property has been purchased, you will receive your portion of the proceeds including any outstanding balance in your capital account.
Am I able to use a 1031 exchange?
Not directly on our platform. If you have an LLC that currently has only one property and is selling that property, you are able to purchase the next property with your 1031 exchange. Once you have purchased it, you can contact us to move it to our platform. It will still have to be owned by the LLC, but you can then start to sell portions of that investment property.
